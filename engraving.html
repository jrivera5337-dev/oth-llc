The Process

How It Works

Step 01

You Submit Your Design

Logo, text, or geometric pattern. We work from your artwork and advise on what translates well to the process.

Step 02

We Model It

Your design is built in modeling software and translated into an energetic layout. This is where the expertise lives — getting the geometry exactly right.

Step 03

We Execute

Energetic materials are applied with precision and initiated. The detonation transfers the image permanently into the metal surface.

Step 04

You Receive the Piece

A finished, one-of-a-kind metal piece — engraved by explosion, documented on video. Nothing else produces a mark quite like it.
