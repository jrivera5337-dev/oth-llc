    Federal Explosives License or Permit — current and valid.

    Notice of Clearance

    Current notice of clearance submitted with your inquiry.

Local LE Requirements

    Certified Breacher on Staff

    Your department must have a certified breacher on staff. Hard requirement. No exceptions.

    Purchase Request on Letterhead

    Formal purchase request on department letterhead.

  • 03

    Follow-Up Call

    An OTH operator will contact you to confirm credentials and discuss pricing and storage.
